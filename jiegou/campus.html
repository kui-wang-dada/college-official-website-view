Campus life

Chinese Government (CSC) and Huazhong Agricultural University (HZAUS) scholarships are available for outstanding international students who graduated from top universities. Chinese Government and Huazhong Agricultural University scholarships cover tuition and accommodation fees and provide monthly living expenses for post-graduation studies.
